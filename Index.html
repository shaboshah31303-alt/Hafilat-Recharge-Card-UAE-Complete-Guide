What Is a Hafilat Card?

The Hafilat Card is a smart payment card used for Abu Dhabi public buses. It allows passengers to pay bus fares electronically without using cash. The system is designed to make public transportation faster, easier, and more secure for residents and visitors in the UAE.

Hafilat Cards are commonly used by office workers, students, tourists, and daily commuters across Abu Dhabi.

Types of Hafilat Cards

Anonymous Card

This card is ideal for regular travelers and tourists. It can be purchased quickly and used immediately for bus travel.

Personalized Card

A personalized Hafilat Card is linked to the user’s information and offers better protection if the card gets lost.

Special Category Cards

Students, senior citizens, and people of determination may receive discounted travel benefits through special Hafilat cards.

How to Check Hafilat Card Balance

Keeping enough balance on your card is important for smooth bus travel. Passengers can check balance through:

  • Online balance check systems
  • Ticket vending machines
  • Recharge centers
  • Public transport customer service offices

Always check your balance before boarding the bus to avoid travel interruptions.

How to Use Hafilat Card on Abu Dhabi Buses

Tap While Entering

Place the card on the card reader when entering the bus.

Tap While Exiting

Tap the card again when leaving the bus so the correct fare can be deducted automatically.

Incorrect tapping may result in additional fare deductions.

Common Hafilat Card Problems

Low Balance

Passengers may not be able to travel if the available balance is insufficient.

Card Damage

Physical damage can stop the card from working properly on bus machines.

Recharge Delay

Some online recharge transactions may take time to update in the transport system.

Important Tips for Hafilat Users

  • Always maintain enough balance
  • Recharge before long trips
  • Protect the card from damage
  • Use official recharge channels only
  • Tap correctly during entry and exit
