What Is a Hafilat Card?

The Hafilat Card is a smart travel card used for public buses in Abu Dhabi. Instead of paying cash, passengers use the card to pay bus fares electronically. It helps make travel faster, easier, and more convenient for daily commuters.

The card is widely used by workers, students, residents, and tourists across Abu Dhabi public transport services.

Types of Hafilat Cards

Anonymous Hafilat Card

This card is suitable for regular users and tourists. It can be purchased easily and used immediately for bus travel.

Personalized Hafilat Card

A personalized card contains the user’s information and offers better security if the card is lost.

Student and Senior Cards

Special Hafilat cards are available for students, senior citizens, and people of determination. These cards may provide discounted fares.

How to Check Hafilat Card Balance

Checking your balance regularly helps avoid travel interruptions during bus journeys.

  • Check balance online
  • Use ticket vending machines
  • Visit service centers
  • Check through recharge systems

Always maintain enough balance before starting your journey.

How to Use the Hafilat Card

While Boarding

Tap the card on the bus card reader when entering the bus.

While Exiting

Tap the card again before leaving the bus so the correct fare can be calculated.

Failing to tap properly may result in extra charges.

Benefits of Using Hafilat Card

  • Easy online recharge options
  • Faster boarding process
  • Secure travel system
  • Convenient for daily commuters
  • Useful for tourists visiting Abu Dhabi

Common Hafilat Card Problems

Low Balance

Passengers may not be allowed to travel if the card balance is insufficient.

Card Not Working

Damaged cards or system errors can sometimes create issues during travel.

Recharge Delay

Online recharge may occasionally take some time to appear in the system.

Important Travel Tips

  • Recharge your card before long journeys
  • Protect the card from physical damage
  • Use official recharge channels only
  • Check balance regularly
  • Always tap in and tap out properly

Difference Between Hafilat and Nol Card

Many people in the UAE confuse the Hafilat Card with the Nol Card.

  • Hafilat Card: Used mainly for Abu Dhabi public buses
  • Nol Card: Used in Dubai for metro, buses, and trams
